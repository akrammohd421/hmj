Professional and
dedicated consulting services

A service and maintenance contract could Reduce risks of failure of equipment when the need arises – saving your investments and your business. Extended life from your equipment due to preventive maintenance visits. Compliance with local fire office and insurance company requirements. We offer a single maintenance contract for all the fire safety equipments / systems installed in your premises – portable fire extinguishers, Water based Fire fighting system, Gas based automatic flooding systems, Emergency lighting, Emergency Evacuation signages etc. There are clear advantages by going for a single contract. The most important is the peace of mind that will come when you know that there will be no gaps or overlaps in your fire protection due to various agencies sharing different tasks.

What we do Under
Aannual Maintenance Contract

  • Inspections render breakdown maintenance and monthly preventive maintenance during normal working hours to the equipment.
  • Service calls in a month with a Response time of 24 hours during normal business working hours if a call is placed before 10 AM else by next working day.
  • If the valued client is finding difficulty in the viewing of live and recorded images and controlling of all cameras by the authorized users present in the LAN and internet the same shall be supported by the service engineer of the company within the same working day.
  • If in the middle of the contract period, if any of the parties is not satisfied due to some valid reason, they can terminate the contract after giving one month prior notice in writing and the balance charges, if applicable - will be refunded to the Customer by the Service Contractor at depending on the reason provided by the customer.
